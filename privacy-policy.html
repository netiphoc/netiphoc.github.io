Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

J1 Studio We operates the mobile application Beat-Engine. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information (like your name or email) directly within the game. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you for advertising and analytics purposes.

2. Third-Party Services

The game uses third-party services that may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. These include:

  • Google Play Services: Used for game progress and achievements.

  • Unity LevelPlay / ironSource: Used for delivering advertisements (Interstitials, Rewarded Video, etc.).

  • Unity Analytics: Used to understand how players interact with the game to improve performance.

3. Advertising and Data Collection

Ad networks may collect your Advertising ID (GAID) and device information to provide personalized ads. By using this app, you consent to the collection of this data by our ad partners (Unity/ironSource).

4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

5. GDPR / International Users

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) have certain data protection rights. We take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data through the settings provided by the third-party ad providers in the app.
